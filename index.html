About Bio-CFD Foundation

The Bio-CFD Foundation is committed to leveraging computational fluid dynamics (CFD) to enhance the understanding of biological systems, focusing on human health, environmental impact, and complex fluid dynamics in living organisms. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical research and real-world biological applications.

Our Research

  • Human Hemodynamics: Exploring blood flow in the human body to better understand cardiovascular health.
  • Environmental Fluid Dynamics: Studying how fluids interact in ecosystems and impact environmental sustainability.
  • Cellular Microfluidics: Investigating fluid behavior at the cellular level to improve drug delivery systems and disease modeling.

Collaboration Opportunities

We welcome collaboration with universities, research institutes, and industry partners who share our vision of applying CFD to biological systems. Our expertise in simulation and modeling can accelerate breakthroughs in fields such as medicine, biotechnology, and environmental sciences.
